Privacy Policy

Overview

CardVaultAU ("the App") is developed and maintained by Irradiated Solution. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device using UserDefaults and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Your Pokémon card collection (card names, quantities, prices)
  • Price history records for your cards
  • App preferences and Pro subscription status

Camera

The App requests camera access solely to photograph Pokémon cards for name recognition (OCR). Photos are processed entirely on-device using Apple's Vision framework. No images are uploaded, stored, or shared with any third party.

Third-Party Services

Pokémon TCG API

Card search results are fetched from the public Pokémon TCG API. Your search queries (card names) are sent to this API. Please review their privacy policy at pokemontcg.io.

Google AdMob

The free version of CardVaultAU displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalised ads. For full details, see Google's Privacy Policy. Upgrading to Pro removes all advertisements.

Apple StoreKit / App Store

In-app purchases (Pro subscriptions) are processed by Apple via StoreKit 2. Irradiated Solution does not collect or store any payment information. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not have user accounts or cloud storage
  • We do not sell, share, or rent any data to third parties
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not store or transmit any photos taken in the app

Children's Privacy

CardVaultAU is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

Data Retention & Deletion

All card collection data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling CardVaultAU from your device. We have no ability to access or recover this data once the app is removed.

Your Rights (Australian Privacy Act)

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal information we hold about you. As we do not collect personal information beyond what is described above, these rights primarily apply to data held by our third-party service providers (Google AdMob, Pokémon TCG API).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
